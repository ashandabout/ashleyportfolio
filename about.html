About

Ashley Gifford is a photographer, writer, and curator based in Portland, OR.

She is also learning to code using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

Bio

Ashley is the Founder & Director of Art & About PDX, a platform documenting art happenings in the Pacific Northwest. A new collaborative project—Critical Viewing has evolved as Ashley’s art practice has grown to include creating small-batch art publications. Additionally she is the co-founder & co-director of homebase, an alternative art space in SE Portland.She attended the University of Hawaii at Manoa with degrees in Art History & Studio Art, and an emphasis in Curatorial Studies. Ashley works full-time as a Software Project Manager for a networking company and loves to explore the intersection between technology and art, which is why she is learning to code.
