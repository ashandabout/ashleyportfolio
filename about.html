bio

Ashley Gifford is a photographer, writer, and curator based in Portland, OR. She is the co-founder of homebase and Founder & Creative Director of Art & About PDX, a platform documenting art happenings in Portland since 2014. Ashley’s art practice has grown to now include creating small-batch art publications, which has evolved into a new collaborative project—Critical Viewing. She attended the University of Hawaii at Manoa with degrees in Art History & Studio Art, and an emphasis in Curatorial Studies. Ashley is a Project Manager for a networking company and loves to explore the intersection between technology and art.
